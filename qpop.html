Qpop

Ninety One or 91 (kaz. "Tosan bir", rus. "Ninety one") is a Kazakhstani boy band that debuted on September 1, 2015 under the label JUZ Entertainment, which includes: A.Z. (Azamat Zenkaev), ALEM (Batyrkhan Malikov), ACE (Azamat Ashmakyn), ZaQ (Dulat Mukhametkaliev) and BALA (Daniyar Kulumshin). The founders of the new musical genre Q-pop (English Qazaq-pop, Kazakh pop), which originates from K-pop. The producer of the group is a member of the Kazakhstani musical group ORDA - Erbolat Bedelkhan.
