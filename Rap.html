Truwer

Truwer is one of the most famous Kazakh rappers. The hip-hop performer was born on July 17, 1994 in the city of Pavlodar. During his school years he was engaged in karate. At the age of 18, Truwer suffered a serious injury, which forced him to stop practicing martial arts. The rapper recorded the first tracks in 2015. A huge impetus for his career was given by his participation in the Jillzay group together with Scryptonite and 104. The album of the musical group 718 Jungle was especially successful. Truwer has a number of Scryptonite feats, which positively influenced the popularity of the Kazakh. In 2020 Truwer released the debut album "Kaz.Pravdy". The tracklist of the disc includes 14 songs with the participation of famous rappers. The debut LP Truwera was highly appreciated by the audience. The rapper is a resident of the Musica 36 label. The fan base of the hip-hop artist is constantly growing, which testifies to the correctness of the chosen path. Almost nothing is known about Truwer's personal life.

104

Real name 104 - Yuri Drobitko. The rapper was born on June 20, 1994 in the city of Pavlodar. 104 began to get involved in hip-hop during his school years. In middle school, he even tried making beats on his own. Yuri reached a more professional level after the creation of the Jillzay group. The Kazakhstani rapper gained popularity after the release of the Scryptonite album "House with Normal Phenomena", where he took part in the recording of several feats with Adil. 104 revealed his talent as a rapper on Jillzay's album 718 Jungle. In 2017, Yuri, together with his colleague Truwer, released a joint album "Safari". Initially, the performer recorded songs under the pseudonym Yuri Chetverg. Then he changed his nickname to 104: where 10 is the letter U, and 4 is the letter C. The rapper came to great success after the release of his debut album "Kino Without Cigarettes". The album took first place in the Russian Apple Music chart. In the longplay, you can hear 104 fits with Scryptonite, Truwer, Miyagi, Saluki.

V $ X V Prince

V $ X V Prince was born in 1990 in the city of Almaty. Askhat studied well at school, but he understood that study and work would not play a major role in his life. Despite this, V $ X V Prince attended additional English classes and graduated from the Kazakh State University. As a child, Askhat was inspired by the work of Eminem. The first track #selfmade by V $ X V Prince was recorded in 2015. It was followed by a number of cool songs: "Smoke smoke", "Fashionable girlfriend", "It hurts, but not enough." On the wave of success, Askhat organizes the VERNYY group. The musical group brought popularity to the Kazakhstani rapper both at home and abroad. Captown

In 2020, the Kazakh rap scene continued to actively gain momentum. The discovery of the year was the Captown group from the capital of Kazakhstan, Astana. The musical group was formed in 2016. Three friends Zaton, Fortygunz and Qantarskii bluez recorded their first tracks. Each of the Captown members has their own original style. Quantarskii bluez is responsible for the band's sound. Fortygunz is famous for its verses. Zaton stands out for its charisma and punchlines. Captown songs are made at the intersection of old and new schools. From old school hip-hop and they inherited the meaning in songs, from newschool - vibe. Popularity came to Kazakhs in 2018 after the release of the track "What did you think". A snippet of the song went viral on social media, dramatically increasing the group's following. In early 2020, Captown released their mini album January. In September, the group released its debut LP "Illusion of Normality" featuring Lizer, Black Seed Oil and Levan Gorozia. Popular rappers liked the music of Captown so much that they themselves wanted to record feats with Kazakhs!

Skryptonite

Scryptonite is the most famous Kazakhstani rapper. The hip-hop artist was born on June 3, 1990. The real name of Scryptonite is Adil. In 2009, he formed the JILLZ group with his friends. The work of the musical group was popular, and its members 104, Truwer and Niman later became famous rappers. In 2013, Scryptonite released the first big hit VBVVCTND. After that, the Gazgolder production center became interested in Adil, the hip-hop performer eventually became a resident of it. At the age of 25, Scryptonite released his debut album "House with Normal Phenomena". Thanks to him, Adil loudly declared himself in the music arena, and many publications called him the rapper of the year. In 2017, Scryptonite released two albums at once: "Holiday on 36 Street" and "Ouroboros". After that, he announced that he was quitting rap. Instead, Adil decides to deal with the release of diverse music and creates a musical group Gruppa Skryptonite.
